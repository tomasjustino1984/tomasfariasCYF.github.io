Brigadeiro

Brigadeiro are a classic Brazilian treat similar to chocolate truffles that are extremely scrumptious and delicious. This sweet is loved here in Brazil.

  • Time: 00:15m
  • Serves: 20
Ingredients Quantity
Sweetened condensed milk 200ml
butter 100g
cocoa powder 100g
chocolate sprinkles 100g

Pudim

I learned to make this Brazilian-style flan (pudim de leite condensado) when we lived in Brazil. It has a wonderful creamy texture without being too heavy. The melted syrup will run down the sides, forming a caramel sauce. Serve cool with a dollop of whipped cream on the side.

  • Time: 03h:15m
  • Serves: 08
Ingredients Quantity
Sweetened condensed milk 200ml
milk 200ml
white sugar 100g
eggs 5

Step 1: How to Do:

Click on the button next to it: Pudim
